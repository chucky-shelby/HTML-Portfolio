My top 3 movies of the year.

1.Guardians of the Galaxy: Vol.3

Still reeling from the loss of Gamora, Peter Quill must rally his team to defend the universe and protect one of their own.
If the mission is not completely successful, it could possibly lead to the end of the Guardians as we know them.
another scinitillating piece from the very talented director, James Gunn

2. Extraction 2

Tasked with extracting a family who is at the mercy of a Georgian gangster,
Tyler Rake infiltrates one of the world's deadliest prisons in order to save them.
But when the extraction gets hot, and the gangster dies in the heat of battle,
his equally ruthless brother tracks down Rake and his team to Sydney, in order to get revenge.

3. John Wick: Chapter 4

With the price on his head ever increasing, John Wick uncovers a path to defeating The High Table.
But before he can earn his freedom, Wick must face off against a new enemy with powerful alliances across the globe,
and forces that turn old friends into foes. 3 hours of immense action and unrivalled entertainment

4. Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

Optimus Prime and the Autobots take on their biggest challenge yet.
When a new threat capable of destroying the entire planet emerges, they must team up with a powerful faction of Transformers,
known as the Maximals to save Earth.
